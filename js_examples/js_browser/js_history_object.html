JS examples: history object

    A brief history of URLs in your browswer window
  • You can use JS to show the number of items in your history list
  • history.length;
  • If it only shows 1; go somewhere and come back
    History back button
  • You can make a back button
  • You will need information in your history list
    History forward button
  • You can make a forward button
  • You will need information in your history list
    Go to a specific number in history list
  • You can specify where to go in history list
  • Positive numbers go forward
  • Negative numbers go backward
  • In order for this to work you will need to seed your history list
  • 2 places back and 2 places forward
    History explanation
  • window.history object is the container for the browser's history
  • the window. portion can be omitted
  • It his it's limitations to protect privacy of users
  • history.back() - same as clicking back in the browser
  • The history.back() method loads the previous URL in the history list.
  • history.forward() - same as clicking forward in the browser
  • The history forward() method loads the next URL in the history list.
